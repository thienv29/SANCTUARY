Welcome to Sanctuary

Your Very Own Paradise

Less than two hour drive from Ho Chi Minh City, you arrive into fresh unpolluted seaside air and breezes of Ho Tram. Sanctuary Villas combine easy accessibility with peaceful beachfront seclusion, a perfect second home surrounded by nature.

The Lifestyle

Sanctuary Ho Tram has become one of the finest residential communities, combining the most exclusive beachfront and riverfront villas, with a wide range of exquisite lifestyle amenities.

The site of 17 Hectares has a 800 meter long white sandy beach as well as a natural replenishing fresh water lake. Villas owners will enjoy the clean sea air and sweeping vistas of the East Sea. Phases 1 and 2 were developed 2012 and 2017 and comprised 55 luxury villas of various sizes which has created a diverse up market residential seaside community of satisfied investors.
